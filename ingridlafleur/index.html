Ingrid LaFleur

A really intriguing outer-boxer

Sculpture "The People's Mayor", 2016
When I first saw this piece I thought the book was a Christian bible. When I looked closer and saw that it wasn't I figured, well, if there is any book that needs to be destroyed and become something else, it's the bible.

I've never seen Borax used. The crystalized preservation of her sculptural objects is beautiful and very fantasy-like. They could be set props in The Neverending Story or Legend.

Questions
Do participants in your performances understand what you are doing, what you are communicating? Do they understand what their role is?
